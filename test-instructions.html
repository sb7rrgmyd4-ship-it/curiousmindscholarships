📋 General Instructions

Follow these steps throughout your assessment session.

  1. You will complete three separate timed tests in one session.
  2. Tests must be taken in order: Test 1 → Test 2 → Test 3.
  3. The timer starts the moment questions appear on screen.
  4. You may navigate between questions before submitting each test.
  5. Once submitted, you cannot return to a completed test.
  6. You must pass all three tests (minimum 60% each) to proceed.
  7. Questions are generated specifically for your course and level.

⚠️ Anti-Malpractice Policy

The following actions during an active test session will result in immediate automatic disqualification.

Switching to another browser tab or window
Minimising or leaving the browser
Exiting fullscreen mode during the test
Right-clicking anywhere on the test page
Using blocked shortcuts (Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, F12, etc.)
Using a secondary screen without approval
Refreshing or closing the page during the test
Attempting to screenshot or print the test
Academic integrity reminder: The actions listed above violate our honour code and may result in disqualification if reported. By proceeding, you agree to complete all tests honestly and without external assistance. Serious violations may be reported to your institution at the Foundation's sole discretion.
